Our Services

Tailored Recruitment Solutions for Private Equity Firms, Family Offices & Special Project Needs

  • C-Suite Search

    We specialize in recruiting exceptional C-level executives for private equity-backed companies across all sectors and growth stages. Our services include placements for Chief Executive Officers (CEOs), Chief Financial Officers (CFOs), Chief Operating Officers (COOs), Chief Technology Officers (CTOs), and other C-suite roles critical to driving value creation.

  • Board Services

    In addition to executive roles, we provide comprehensive board recruitment services, including identifying and evaluating candidates for board of director and advisory board positions. Our extensive network allows us to source top-tier board talent aligned with your strategic objectives.

  • Executive Assessment

    Ensuring the right leadership fit is crucial. Our in-depth executive assessment process evaluates candidates' leadership capabilities, cultural alignment, and potential for success within your unique organizational context.

  • Private Equity Special Projects

    We understand that private equity firms often require specialized talent for specific initiatives such as operational value creation, carve-outs, integrations, and turnarounds. Our team can rapidly source and place experienced executives and subject matter experts to lead these critical projects.

  • Family Office Recruitment

    For family offices, we offer tailored recruitment solutions for executive roles within the office as well as leadership positions for the family's operating companies and new investment opportunities. Our discreet approach ensures confidentiality throughout the process.
